About Bright Capital

Built on Conviction.
Driven by Pipeline.

Bright Capital was founded on a single belief: the most valuable thing you can bring to a multifamily deal is a deal worth doing.

The Story

From Learning the
Business to Building
the Platform.

I spent time working alongside operators and investors in the multifamily space — watching how deals got sourced, structured, and closed. That experience made one thing clear: the operators who scale aren't necessarily the best operators. They're the ones who never run out of deals to look at.

I built Bright Capital to fill that gap — not as a fund, not as a broker, but as a deal sourcing and underwriting partner for operators who are ready to grow but don't have the infrastructure to prospect at scale.

My focus is Texas and the broader Sunbelt. These are markets I know — where I've sourced, underwritten, and closed alongside capital partners. The pipeline is active. The relationships are real. And the model is simple: I find the deal, you close it, we win together.

Bright Capital's first closed deal, The Jackson in Lubbock, TX, is the proof of concept. 86 units, a deep value entry, and a clear value-add thesis — sourced, underwritten, and delivered. That's what we do, and we're just getting started.

How We Operate

The Principles Behind
Every Deal We Bring.

Quality Over Volume

We don't flood your inbox with questionable deals to look active. Every opportunity we send has passed a strict internal filter. Your time is too valuable for anything less.

Skin in the Game

We take GP equity in every deal we bring. That's not a formality — it means we're underwriting with our own money on the line. If the deal doesn't work, neither do we.

Operator-First Mindset

We're not here to compete with operators. We're here to support them. Our job is to make the operator's job easier by removing the sourcing burden so you can focus on execution.

Texas & Sunbelt Depth

We don't try to cover every market. We go deep in the geographies we know — where our relationships are strongest and we can identify opportunities before they're broadly marketed.
